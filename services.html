Our Services

What we can do for you

Markets

We are providing a close formal markets to smallholder farmers so that they can make a sound profit from their hard work.

Livelihood equality affects 50 to 70 percent of Malawians and due to the challenge of live hood equity, small holder farmers fail to produce enough food to supply or satisfy each and every house hold due to limited finances that fail to enable them access more productive inputs for their limited land. This result as they fail to access formal markets to transportation cost and other issues hence they sell their products to vendors who pay unreasonable low price. A close formal market to these rural small holder farmers, help the farmers to make a sound profit for their hard working and improve their livelihood equity.

Skill Development

We are providing a package and training on modern ways of production to smallholder farmers so that they can improve their productivity.

To increase the productivity, we offer training to the farmers on modern ways for producing crops so that they can produce more on the limited land they have. Not only that but also other methods of dealing with climate change challenges like pest, soil salinity and others

Proper Storage

We provide proper storage as a means of reducing post-harvest loss in order to increase food supply on the market.

Due to lack of knowledge and proper infrastructure of the small holder farmers to prevent losses due to: pest, spoilage and improper storage. Most of the food that is harvested is put into waste rather than consumption. This result into low productivity, we offer proper storage of the products after purchasing from the farmers to increase the productivity and food supply on the market.

Climate Change & Agriculture Solutions

We provide climate change smart agriculture solutions to smallholder farmers to increase their productivity.

Climate change pose much impact on food supply because of high dependence on drought sensitive crops. Drought produces a conducive environment for breeding and multiplication of pest. Pest also play a big role in reducing the productivity of agriculture products. We provide solutions to climate changes that lead to poor productivity.
